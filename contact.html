Frequently Asked Questions

Is IMAKSA RERA licensed?+
Yes, IMAKSA is fully licensed by the Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) in Dubai. All our agents are RERA certified and operate under Dubai Land Department regulations.
Can I invest in Dubai real estate as a foreigner?+
Absolutely. Dubai allows 100% foreign ownership in designated freehold areas. This includes Palm Jumeirah, Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, Business Bay, and many more prime locations.
What is the Golden Visa and how do I qualify?+
The UAE Golden Visa grants 10-year residency. You qualify by purchasing property worth AED 2 million or more. IMAKSA handles the entire application process for you.
What fees are involved in buying property in Dubai?+
Typical costs include 4% DLD transfer fee, 2% agency fee, and AED 4,000 admin fee. We provide full transparency on all costs before you commit to anything.
Do you offer property management services?+
Yes. We offer full property management including tenant sourcing, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and annual reporting — so you earn passive income stress-free.
